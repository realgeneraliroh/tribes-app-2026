# 🏛️ Co-Op Governance Is Here. Your First Vote Is Ready.

Tribes is a co-op. That's always been the thesis: you're not a product, you're a stakeholder. Starting today, that's not just a sentence on the About page. It's real infrastructure.

## What's New

We built a governance system. If you're a paid co-op member, you can now:

- **Vote on platform proposals** at [Governance](/voting)
- **Submit your own proposals** if you've founded a tribe
- **Debate proposals** in threaded discussion threads before you vote

This is how the platform evolves. Not a founder making unilateral calls, but the community deciding together what Tribes becomes.

## Your First Proposal: NSFW Content Policy

The first item on the table is our adult content policy. It covers:

- **Where NSFW content is allowed:** inside dedicated, clearly marked Tribes that are automatically locked to Private and E2E encrypted
- **How moderation works with encryption:** a reporting tool that lets members flag illegal content without breaking privacy for everyone else
- **Age verification:** how we plan to comply with expanding state laws without building surveillance infrastructure
- **The hard line:** zero direct monetization of adult content, and platform survival always comes first

This isn't a done deal. It's a draft. We want your input, your objections, and your vote.

Head to [Governance](/voting) to read the full proposal and cast your vote.
